About Takemichi Animes

Takemichi Animes is a group of people who like anime and think it is more fun when we share Takemichi Animes with each other. Our website is where fans can go to find, share and talk about anime from over the internet. We like to think that Takemichi Animes is a place where people can come together and share their love for anime.

What You Will Find Here:

Anime News

Our group helps find the news that anime fans want to know about anime. We do not change the news to sound better. We just share what our group finds about anime.

Video Collection

You can find videos of anime, music videos, clips and cool moments that other fans of anime share. If you find something you like you can share it with our group of anime fans.

Fan Dubs Gallery

We like to show the things our group of anime fans can do. You can look at videos that fans made and hear how much they love anime. This is a way to see what other fans of anime are doing.

Third-Party Resources

We have links to websites that can help you with anime. Our group finds these links. Shares them with everyone who likes anime.
